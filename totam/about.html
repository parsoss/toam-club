About Us

We are an club for the first year of Bansong Girls' Middle School in Changwon. It consists of a total of 10 students and is intended to improve the researching and discussion skills of the first graders.

Creative & Make Perfect

We research a variety of scientific issues, environmental issues, and social issues.
Due to the COVID-19 issue, we are meeting online.

Our Vision

Let's develop understanding and persuasion through discussion!

Our Mission

Let's improve science skills through exploration!

Our Goal

Let's work hard for a better future!
